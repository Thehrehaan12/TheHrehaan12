Recipe #1 (Chocolate Cake)

  • Time: 45 mins
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 1 3/4 cups
sugar 2 cups
salt 1/2 teaspoon
baking powder 1 1/2 teaspoons
baking soda 1 1/2 teaspoons
milk 1 cup
eggs 2
vegetable oil 1/2 cup
vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
water (boiling) 1 cup

Process

Step 1:Gather the ingredients. Preheat your oven to 350 F.

Step 2:Butter and flour two 9-inch cake pans. The cakes will release better if you line the bottoms of the pans with a circle of parchment paper.

Step 3:In a large mixing bowl, sift the flour, sugar, and cocoa powder. Add the salt, baking powder, and baking soda, and whisk until everything is blended.

Step 4:In a separate mixing bowl, combine the milk, eggs, oil, and vanilla, and whisk to combine. Then whisk in the boiling water.

Step 5:Add the wet ingredients to the dry and stir until combined.

Step 6:Pour the batter into your prepared pans and transfer them to the oven.

Step 7:Bake for about 30 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean, or with a crumb or two attached. Start testing after 27 minutes to avoid over-baking.

Step 8:Cool the cakes for 10 minutes in the pans on a wire rack. Loosen the edges by running a knife along the sides, turn the cakes out onto the racks, and cool for at least another hour.

Step 9:Once cool, frost with your favorite chocolate frosting recipe.

Recipe #2 (Vanilla Cake)

  • Time:1 hr 45 mins
  • Serves: 10

Process

Ingredients Quantity
cooking spray
all-purpose flour 2 ⅔ cups
white sugar 1 cup
baking powder 1 tablespoon
vanilla extract 1 tablespoon
salt 2 pinches
eggs 3
milk ¾ cup
vegetable oil ¾ cup

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9-inch cake tin with cooking spray and line with parchment paper.

Step 2:Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, vanilla extract, and salt together in a bowl. Add eggs, milk, and vegetable oil. Mix by hand or use an electric mixer on low speed until smooth. Add more flour if batter is too runny. Pour into the prepared pan.

Step 3:Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of the cake comes out clean, about 1 hour.

Step 4:Remove from the oven and let cool, 15 to 30 minutes. Transfer to the refrigerator to chill slightly, 15 to 30 minutes more. Slice the cooled cake through the middle to make 2 layers.

Recipe #3 (Chocolate Cookies)

  • Time:1 hr
  • Serves: 24
Ingredients Quantity
butter, softened 1 cup
white sugar 1 cup
packed brown sugar 1 cup
eggs 2
vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
baking soda 1 teaspoon
hot water 2 teaspoons
salt ½ teaspoon
all-purpose flour 3 cups
semisweet chocolate chips 2 cups
chopped walnuts 1 cup

Process

Step 1:Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).

Step 2:Cream together the butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until smooth. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to batter along with salt. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and nuts. Drop by large spoonfuls onto ungreased pans.

Step 3:Bake for about 10 minutes in the preheated oven, or until edges are nicely browned.
